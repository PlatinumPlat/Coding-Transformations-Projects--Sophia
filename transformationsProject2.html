Graph one's heartbeat over the course of 10 minutes.

BPM Measurements

Enter the amount of beats you measured for each minute.

Naturally, the range for each of your inputs is 0 to 240, in the units of BPM (beats per minute). Notice that any measurement that exceeds or is lower than the (already extreme) range of 40 BPM to 220 BPM is not healthy and any individual which measures this rate should attempt to retake their measurement to determine its accuracy. If your measurement persists, please consult a professional and seek immediate medical attention.
